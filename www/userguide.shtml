A User's Guide to The Anatomy of the Laboratory Mouse by Margaret J. Cook


Introduction

The purpose of this User's Guide is to assist you in using the electronic version of The Anatomy of the Laboratory Mouse by Margaret J. Cook.

Navigation

There are several ways to navigate through this book:

  • The Table of Contents
  • The Previous and Next links on most pages
  • The Subject Index
  • The Image Index

Special Features

Many anatomical terms in the Subject Index are linked to the appropriate term in the Adult Mouse Anatomy browser at MGI.
